# Cookie Policy

## What are cookies

Cookies are small text files that websites store on your device to remember information about your preferences.

## Cookies we use

### Essential cookies
- **consent_preferences** — stores your consent choice for cookie usage
- **session_security** — ensures session security

### Analytics cookies (requires consent)
- **analytics_anonymized** — anonymous visit statistics to improve the website

## Managing cookies

You can:
- Change settings through the consent banner
- Disable cookies in browser settings
- Delete existing cookies

## Third-party services

When playing video or audio content, cookies from external platforms (YouTube, SoundCloud) may be loaded. These cookies are loaded only after your consent to view the content.

## Updates

Changes to the cookie policy are published on this page.
