# AI Transparency Statement

*In accordance with EU AI Act, Article 50*

## AI Usage in Content Creation

### Editorial Process
- **Research and analysis**: Automated tools are used for searching and processing open data
- **Content drafts**: AI may be applied for creating initial material drafts
- **Editing**: All materials undergo human editing and verification

### Quality Control
- **Human oversight**: Every published material is reviewed and approved by a human editor
- **Fact-checking**: Factual statements are verified from independent sources
- **Responsibility**: Final responsibility for content lies with the FolkUp editorial team

## Analytical Tools

### Data Processing
- Automated systems may be used for analyzing large volumes of open data
- Analysis results are always interpreted and verified by experts

### Personalization
- The website does not use AI for content personalization or targeting
- Analytics are anonymous and do not create user profiles

## Limitations and Risks

### Known Limitations
- AI systems may generate inaccuracies, which are eliminated by human control
- Automated analysis may miss nuances requiring expert assessment

### Precautionary Measures
- Multi-stage verification of all materials
- Transparent indication of sources and methodology
- Regular updates to quality control procedures

## User Rights

You have the right to:
- Know when interacting with an AI system
- Receive explanations of automated decisions
- Contact a human operator for review
